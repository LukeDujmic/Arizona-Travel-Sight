Environmental Activities in Peoria

There are several activities that can be done solely based on the exquisite environment of Peoria.

Visiting Lake Pleasant

Just here for the view and relaxation of a beautiful lake in a desert! Going to lake pleasant for a cruise will certainly aid you in this adventure. With narration, dining and music, cruises on lake pleasant before a sunset can be an unexpectedly amazing experience.

New River Trail

Want to go along a 13 mile path for lots of possibilities! Well by visiting the New River Trail, several opportunities open up. The extesive path is dedicated to running, walking, bike riding, viewing or whatever else you can envision. It may just seem simple, but this 13 mile path allows people to see a few of the great features in the geography and structure of the city.

Visiting Zoos

Of all places, you wouldn't expect Arizona or even Peoria, a hotter city, to feature astounding zoos. The World Wildlife Zoo is one of the best in the area, as it is Arizona's largest collection of exotic animals. You can witness the beauty of nature across various regions of South America and Africa. Activities in the zoo ranges from petting certain animals to riding boats, skyrides and trains.
